Handy Shark Games

Description:

“You do not recognize the girl in the mirror…”

In Memories Not Found you take on the role of a girl who wakes up one day seemingly unable to remember who she is, with only a cryptic note left to guide her. Explore a variety of different worlds,
fight unique enemies, and make friends with a quirky cast of characters all while trying to uncover the dark secrets of your past.
However, not everyone you meet may be on your side. How far are you willing to go to remember who you are?

  • From a vast desert to a 24/7 dance party, explore more than a dozen different worlds.
  • Immerse yourself in a soundtrack and art style reminiscent of retro gaming classics.
  • Flee from strange creatures out to get you.
  • Help out those in need of assistance and make friends who
    will prove vital in figuring out the truth of what happened.
  • Conquer the darker parts of yourself as you learn more about your past.
